About the show

Built by fans.
For fans.

The Mint City Soccer Show is Charlotte FC's homegrown podcast — delivering passionate, informed takes on the Crown, Major League Soccer and the beautiful game from supporters who live and breathe it.

From match breakdowns and transfer rumors to player development and club culture, we cover it all.
